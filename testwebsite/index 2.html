I am a Computing Innovation Fellow pursuing post-doctoral research at University of Chicago. My research interests span the broad area of data analytics with particular focus on entity resolution and social network analysis.

I did my undergraduate studies from the Computer Science department of Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) in May, 2014. Prior to joining UMass, I worked as a budding scientist at Xerox Research Centre India, Bangalore for a year.
